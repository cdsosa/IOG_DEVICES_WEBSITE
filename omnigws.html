OMNI-GWS
Tamper Resistant dual Axis Shock Indicator

The Omini-GWS can be used hundreds of times using the included resetting tweezers.

Weatherproofed with stainless steel components to protect against salt spray and fogging on ocean and longer shipments, extended storage or harsh environments.

Tamper resistant design for added security against unauthorized resetting.

Calibrated individually for maximum reliability and accuracy.

Additional Information

Easy Installation & Operation

  1. Set the spring loaded ball sets with coordinating color using tweezers.
  2. When set, make sure the rubber O ring is in place and screw the two pieces together to create the dome. Screw until tight and the small flange
    on the upper piece and the white alignment mark on the bottom piece ,match up.
  3. Insert the security key into the small flange and screw in until tight.
  4. The device is now ready for mounting using screws or adhesive backing.

How It Works

The Omni-GWS is designed with two sets of spring loaded steel balls that become dislodged and fall into a transparent dome when subjected
to an impact that exceeds the preset g-level.

Application

Set the spring loaded ball sets with coordinating color using tweezers. When set, screw together the two transparents pieces to create a dome. Adhere OMNI-GWS to product or shipping box, screw it on to a wooden shipping pallet or mount it on mounting plate and screw on to shipping surface.
