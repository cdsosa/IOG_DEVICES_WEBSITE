Additional Information

Application

Place on a box, on a pallet or on a piece of equipment. Use with companion label for more visibility. Pull tab to start timer. Alerts for possible hidden damage on delivery.

How To Apply

Step 1 - Remove manufacturing from the back of the device

Step 2 - Remove adhesive covering from the back of the device

Step 3 - Place on a box, on a pallet or on a piece of equipment.

Step 4 - Use with companion label for more visibility.

Step 5 - Record Date & Time in Activation Label Area

Step 6 - Pull tab to start timer

How It Works

Is an effective impact indicator that has a visual display that shows date and time when the impact occurs in the transportation chain. Multi-axis response to impact. Surface mounting with easy activation and awareness labels.
